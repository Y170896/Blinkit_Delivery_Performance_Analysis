# Blinkit_Delivery_Performance_Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes delivery operations and customer experience in a quick-commerce environment (Blinkit). The goal is to identify operational inefficiencies, understand customer dissatisfaction, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve performance.

Business Problem
Blinkit operates in a highly competitive quick-commerce industry where delivery speed and service quality directly impact customer satisfaction and retention. However, delays and inconsistent service lead to higher complaints and lower ratings.

Key Insights
	•	Only 28.7% orders are delivered on time
	•	Average delivery delay is approximately 3.6 minutes
	•	Complaint rate is 52.5%
	•	Customer retention rate is 45.6%
	•	Longer delivery times negatively impact customer ratings

Recommendations
	•	Optimize delivery partner allocation during peak hours
	•	Improve logistics efficiency to reduce delays
	•	Strengthen complaint resolution processes
	•	Introduce customer retention programs
